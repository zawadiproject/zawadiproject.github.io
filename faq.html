Frequently Asked Questions

Got a question? We've got answers. If you have other questions, please contact our support team.

How and why does Zawadi use Blockchain?
Our platform manages event tickets as tokens on a public blockchain. This promotes transparency since the tokens can be securely transferred between parties and an immutable record of the encrypted transactions is created and available to the public. The token's assigned unique hash assures that it's not just a copy of the original ticket that is being transferred but it is in fact the ticket itself. This eliminates the transfer of fake or duplicate event tickets therefore ensuring that users of our platform have a seamless and fraud-free experience all from the convenience of their smartphones.
What type of bank cards do you accept?
We accept Visa, MasterCard and American Express
How do I transfer tickets?
If you want to send a ticket to your friend or family member, click the ‘my tickets’ tab on the app and then the event which you wish to transfer tickets for. Click the ‘send’ ticket button. This will open a page where you can either scan the QR code of the address you wish to send tickets to, or you can manually key in the address of the recipient. Make sure you double-check the address you are sending to before you send the tickets.
How do I receive a ticket from another person?
To receive a ticket from your friend or family member, all you need is your wallet address. This can be found on the profile tab in the app. Your friend can either scan the QR code associated with your wallet, or you can copy the wallet address and send it to your friend who can then use it to send you the event ticket
How do I back up my ticket wallet?
You have an option to back up your wallet during account creation. You can choose to back it up straight away or later. If you choose to back up your wallet straight away, your backup phrase will be displayed on the screen. All you need to do is record the backup phrase (whether it be digitally or written down) and store it securely. You will be asked to confirm your backup phrase on the next page to ensure that you have saved it. It’s very important to save the backup phrase in a secure place.
